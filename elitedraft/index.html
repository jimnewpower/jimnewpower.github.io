The essential tool for your home draft...since 2010

Track your entire fantasy football live draft with ease, with support for standard, PPR, dynasty, auction, etc. Customize your player rankings from our frequently updated default rankings (we provide rankings for both standard and PPR formats). Depth charts, current player news, bye weeks, schedules, etc.

During your draft, view player stats, bye weeks, adp, current adp value, auction value, and more; you may set up a player queue, view cheat sheet by all players or by position, view draft results by round or by team, and sort player list multiple ways. Undo picks if you make a mistake, or edit the entire draft, replacing, dropping or inserting picks. Save drafts and come back to them later. Export draft results in .csv or .html format and share them with anyone.
