Track your entire fantasy football draft with ease. Specify league name, number of teams, number of rounds, your draft position, rankings to use (standard, PPR, or your own custom rankings), draft order type (serpentine, 3rd round reversal, etc.), auction draft, optional keeper league options to protect players, etc.

During your draft, view player stats, bye weeks, adp, current adp value, auction value; you may set up a player queue, view cheat sheet by all players or by position, view draft results by round or by team, and sort player list multiple ways. Undo picks if you make a mistake, or edit the entire draft, replacing, dropping or inserting picks. Save drafts and come back to them later. Export draft results in .csv or .html format.
